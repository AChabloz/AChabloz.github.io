The London Forum hosted internationally-acclaimed speakers, including British historian David Irving, American director of the Institute for Historical Review Mark Weber and exiled French revisionist Vincent Reynouard. Other prominent American speakers to appear were Counter Current's Greg Johnson, Andrew Anglin of The Daily Stormer and Jared Taylor, Chairman of American Renaissance.

German nationalist dissidents Gunther Deckert and Albert Schaefer also made appearances, alongside veteran British nationalist Mike Walsh, as well as Mark Collett, leader of Patriotic Alternative.
